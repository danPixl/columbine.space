Privacy Statement

DNS

    For our DNS service, we must maintain a collection of your data:

  • your GitHub profile, to contact if we make changes to the DNS records
  • the address of your website, to know where to redirect to
  • the TXT record, in the case of Google Sites, to verify your site with Google
  • a link to the repo, in the case of GitHub pages, to suggest changes to enhance the experience

News

    For our news service, we must maintain a collection of your data:

  • your name, in order to identify who posted the information

Tracking

    To manage our presence, we use Google Analytics. Here's what that uses:

  • Cookies, which are tiny files on your computer, to identify if you're a repeat visitor or not
  • your IP address, which is how we know who you are, as well as information on what internet service provider you use
  • your browser's user agent, which tells us what browser you're using, the operating system you're using, the screen size of your device, what browser version you are using, and what device you're using
